Biography


A neurosurgeon is a highly specialized medical professional who diagnoses and treats disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system through surgical and non-surgical methods. After completing medical school, neurosurgeons undergo extensive training, including a residency program that lasts about seven to eight years.
